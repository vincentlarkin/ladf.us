Senate President
J. Cameron Henry, Jr.

Welcome to the Louisiana Senate's official website. It is an honor and my privilege to serve as Louisiana Senate President. It is my hope that you will use this website as a tool to stay up to date with the Louisiana State Senate including current news, issues, and events here at the State Capitol.

The Louisiana Senate is comprised of 39 senators who are committed to their constituents and to making Louisiana a better place to live. The members of the Louisiana Senate believe that it is important that we make every effort to ensure the citizens of Louisiana are able to participate in the work of our state government and stay informed about important issues facing our state. We are here to serve you and encourage you to share your ideas and suggestions. Thank you for your interest in the legislative process and helping us to improve our great state of Louisiana.
